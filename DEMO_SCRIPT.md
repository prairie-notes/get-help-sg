# Demo video script (target: 2 minutes 35 seconds)

The final video must include spoken audio, remain under three minutes and be uploaded publicly to YouTube.

## Before recording

- Use a clean browser window with notifications, bookmarks, personal accounts and unrelated tabs hidden.
- Record only your own voice; use no music unless you own or have documented permission for it.
- Do not open third-party websites during the recording or display third-party logos unnecessarily.
- Confirm that any use of clinic or organisation names in the product demo complies with the hackathon’s trademark rule.
- Confirm the actual model used before saying “GPT-5.6.”
- Aim for 2:35 so export or upload padding cannot push the video over three minutes.

## 0:00–0:18 — Problem and project

**On screen:** Landing page.

**Audio:** “Finding mental-health support in Singapore can be confusing, especially when someone is already overwhelmed. I built Get Help SG, a privacy-conscious wayfinder that helps a person move from an uncertain first message to a practical local next step.”

## 0:18–0:48 — Guided flow and safety

**On screen:** Enter “I’m feeling depressed,” continue through the safety and support questions.

**Audio:** “The site uses a guided, non-diagnostic flow. It asks what kind of help feels useful, checks whether immediate support should come first, and lets the visitor choose access, funding and relevant challenges. Urgent Singapore support remains available throughout.”

## 0:48–1:16 — Recommendations and privacy

**On screen:** Show recommended resources, then open the privacy explanation.

**Audio:** “Recommendations are matched to the visitor’s answers using deterministic, reviewable rules. The current version does not send their words to an AI service, create an account or save their answers. It clearly explains that this is navigation, not diagnosis.”

## 1:16–1:52 — Clinic finder

**On screen:** Open private care, choose a region, search or filter, shortlist two clinics and compare them.

**Audio:** “For private care, the directory separates psychiatry from psychology, therapy and counselling. Clinics are grouped consistently by region and neighbourhood. People can search, filter, compare addresses, published fees, booking options and opening hours, and follow the original source before booking.”

## 1:52–2:22 — Codex and GPT-5.6

**On screen:** Briefly show the code or commit history, then return to the app.

**Audio template:** “I collaborated with Codex throughout the build. It helped turn the product concept into the guided interface, implement accessibility and safety states, normalise the provider directory, add regression tests and publish the site. [Insert a truthful, specific sentence about work actually completed with GPT-5.6 after confirming the model or documented workflow.] I made the final product decisions on safety boundaries, Singapore classifications, copy and provider data.”

## 2:22–2:35 — Close

**On screen:** Landing page and public URL.

**Audio:** “Get Help SG gives Singapore residents a calmer, clearer starting point while keeping human services and urgent support at the centre. The live demo and repository are available with this submission.”
